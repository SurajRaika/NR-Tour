## Tour Overview

<p class="text-lg leading-relaxed">
Udaipur, known as the "City of Lakes" and the "Venice of the East," is a jewel of Rajasthan. Nestled amidst the Aravalli Hills, this city is famed for its stunning palaces, serene lakes, and rich Mewar heritage. The tour offers an immersive experience into Udaipur’s royal history, vibrant festivals like the Mewar Festival, and bustling markets filled with Rajasthani crafts.
</p>

## Origin & History

<p class="text-lg">
Founded in 1559 by Maharana Udai Singh II, Udaipur was established as the capital of the Mewar Kingdom after the fall of Chittorgarh. The city’s history is steeped in tales of valor, romance, and architectural brilliance. Its lakes, including Lake Pichola and Fateh Sagar, were created to sustain the region, adding to its ethereal beauty.
</p>

## Global Appeal

<p class="text-lg">
Udaipur’s romantic ambiance and architectural splendor draw travelers worldwide. From the iconic Lake Palace to the bustling streets of Hathi Pol, visitors often embrace local traditions, trying on Rajasthani attire and savoring the city’s blend of history and modernity.
</p>
