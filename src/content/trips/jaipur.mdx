## Tour Overview

<p class="text-lg leading-relaxed">
Jaipur, famously known as the Pink City, offers an immersive journey into Rajasthan's royal past. From imposing forts to intricate palaces, every corner tells a tale of history and artistry.
</p>

## Origin & History

<p class="text-lg">
Founded in the 18th century by Maharaja Jai Singh II, Jaipur was one of India's earliest planned cities. With its rich heritage, the city is a living museum showcasing splendid architecture and vibrant traditions.
</p>

## Global Appeal

<p class="text-lg">
Jaipur’s blend of traditional grandeur and modern vibrancy attracts travelers worldwide. Highlights include the monumental Amber Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal, and the famous bazaars filled with exquisite crafts.
</p>

<ul class="list-disc pl-5 text-lg">
